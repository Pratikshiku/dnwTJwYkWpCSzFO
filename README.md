## Medical Service Pharmaceutical Service System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/03d8c52a1266495ea6977d2e414e574b/ghb20250916) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular Villager, Rural Doctor, Administrator.

**Regular Villager Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Announcement Information
5. Medical Map
6. Medical Drugs, Apply for Purchase
7. Message Feedback
8. Personal Center
9. Customer Service Chat
10. Change Password
11. My Health Records
12. My Emergency Incidents
13. My Message Management



**Rural Doctor Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Homepage
4. Announcement Information
5. Medical Map
6. Medical Drugs, Apply for Purchase
7. Message Feedback
8. Personal Center
9. Customer Service Chat
10. Change Password
11. Personal Information
12. Announcement Information Management, Add New Announcement Information
13. Rural Clinic Management, Add New Rural Clinic
14. View Health Records, Download Health Records
15. Emergency Incident Management, Review Emergency Incident Management
16. View Learning and Training
17. View Assessment Information
18. Purchase Information Management
19. Customer Service Management



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Regular Villager Management
4. Rural Doctor Management
5. Announcement Information Management
6. Rural Clinic Management
7. Health Records Management
8. Learning and Training Management
9. Assessment Information Management
10. Medical Map Management
11. Medical Drugs Management
12. Type Information Management
13. Purchase Information Management
14. Message Board Management
15. Administrator Management
16. Carousel Management
